# SmarTelcom — RAN Congestion Playbook (Seed Manual)

## Intent
Reduce congestion on radio access nodes while preserving enterprise slice SLAs.

## Safe Actions
1. Identify congested cells via PRB utilization > 80% for 15 minutes.
2. Apply mild downlink shaping on best-effort QCI/5QI only.
3. Rebalance load to neighbor cells with spare capacity.
4. Tune handover thresholds (+1 to +3 dB) for edge users.

## Forbidden Actions
- Do not shutdown core_router or AMF/UPF control elements.
- Do not delete running-config or startup-config.
- Do not disable firewall or security policies.
- Do not factory-reset any PE or aggregation switch.

## Graduated Autonomy
- Low risk: threshold optimization, metric collection → auto-approve.
- Medium risk: QoS / traffic shaping → HITL recommended.
- High/Critical: control-plane reload, ACL any-any, core shutdown → HITL mandatory or hard block.

## Example Intent Phrases
- "Reduce congestion on RAN-North while preserving SLA for enterprise slice"
- "Optimize threshold for cell edge throughput on gNodeB-441"
